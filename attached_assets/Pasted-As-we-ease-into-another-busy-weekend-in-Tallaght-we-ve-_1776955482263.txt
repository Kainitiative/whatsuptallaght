As we ease into another busy weekend in Tallaght, we've got an array of events lined up that cater to everyone, despite the typically overcast skies. The forecast suggests a weekend where it's wise to come prepared for anything, but don't let that hamper your plans. Embrace the spirit of Tallaght, venture out, and dive into these exciting activities.

Kicking off the weekend on Friday evening, it’s the perfect time to make plans for Saturday at the Tallaght Library's MenderSpace sessions. Whether you're a seasoned seamstress or a beginner looking to fix a beloved piece of clothing, it's a wonderful opportunity for some quality crafting time. You can book a session and work at your own pace while enjoying the cozy environment offered by the library.

Saturday continues to invite creativity with the Adult Drawing Classes at Rua Red, South Dublin Arts Centre. From 3:30 pm to 5 pm, welcome all skill levels to explore their artistic streak under the guidance of seasoned artists. It's a chance to unlock hidden talents or to refine existing skills in a supportive environment.

Families won't want to miss the enchanting Under The Sea Event at The Square on Saturday between noon and 3 pm. With live sea life shows and fun characters like Perla the Mermaid and Captain Rusty, children will be thoroughly entertained. Cap it off with a special screening of "Shark Tale", making for a memorable outing filled with aquatic adventures.

Music lovers can look forward to an evening of nostalgia as the definitive Oasis tribute band, LIVE FOREVER, performs at The Old Mill Tallaght on Saturday. Relive the glory days with hits that defined a generation and experience the electric atmosphere brought to life by passionate performers.

On Sunday, young creatives can flourish at Leabharlann Thamhlachta's Storytelling and Creative Arts event, starting at 3:30 pm. Designed for children aged three and above, it is a delightful mix of story magic followed by hands-on arts and crafts — an experience sure to spark young imaginations.

With all these activities in our vibrant community, there's no shortage of ways to spend your weekend.
